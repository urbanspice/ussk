ABOUT US

About us

EXPLORE MORE ABOUT US

Welcome to Urban Spice, the destination for an unparalleled culinary journey through the diverse and vibrant flavors of East Indian cuisine in the heart of Saskatoon. At Urban Spice, we are more than just a restaurant; we are a celebration of the rich culinary traditions and cultural tapestry of India.

Our Vision

Our vision at Urban Spice is to be a beacon of authenticity, where every bite of our carefully crafted dishes tells a story of the subcontinent. With a commitment to quality, tradition, and innovation, we strive to provide a unique dining experience that ignites the senses and transports our guests to the bustling streets and bustling markets of India.

Our Cuisine

At Urban Spice, our menu is a meticulously curated selection of dishes that showcase the intricate blend of spices, herbs, and techniques that make Indian cuisine world-renowned. Whether you're a seasoned spice enthusiast or a newcomer to this culinary journey, our menu offers a diverse range of options to cater to every palate. From the fiery depths of spicy curries to the fragrant and comforting biryanis and the mouthwatering tandoori specialties, each dish is prepared with love and dedication to preserving the true essence of Indian flavors.

OUR VALUES

RESPECT

We hold respect in the highest regard. It is the foundation of all our relationships, both within our team and with our valued customers. We believe in treating everyone with dignity, empathy, and fairness. We respect the uniqueness of each individual, their opinions, and their backgrounds. This culture of respect is not just a buzzword for us; it's a way of life that fosters a harmonious and inclusive environment.

TEAMWORK

We understand that the best results are achieved when we work together as a cohesive team. Collaboration is at the heart of what we do. Our team members are not just employees; they are partners in our shared mission. We encourage open communication, idea sharing, and active listening. By pooling our diverse skills and perspectives, we create a dynamic and innovative work environment that benefits not only our team but also our customers.

HOSPITALITY

Hospitality is not just a service, but a philosophy we live and breathe. We believe in providing more than just a meal or a product; we offer a warm and welcoming experience to our customers. From the moment you walk through our doors or engage with us online, we strive to make you feel like a cherished guest. Our commitment to hospitality goes beyond transactions; it's about creating memorable moments and lasting connections.
