URBAN
SPICE

Urban Spice is a unique and first-of-its-kind East Indian Restaurant in Saskatoon serving a wide variety of authentic and flavorful dishes that capture the rich culinary traditions of India. Whether you’re a fan of spicy curries, aromatic biryanis, or delectable tandoori delights, our menu is sure to delight your taste buds and transport you to the vibrant streets of India.
